ISAHP 2016 Youth Session FAQ

  1. Who can attend ISAHP 2016 Youth Session?

    ISAHP is for participants aged 11-18 years old. In order to participate a research project must be completed to be shared in the session. See the ideas section below for possible projects.

  2. Are there some sample ideas for folks interested in participating?

    Yes there are! They can be found at Project Ideas.

  3. Can I find a sample of the simplified Pairwise Questionnaire?

    You can get a scan of the handmade questionnaire here. This is a questionnaire to pairwise compare the following work areas to determine a voter's preferences for which area they would like to be responsible for cleaning up (with the help of a few others):

    1. Math/Supply Shelf
    2. Animals/Science Areas
    3. Library/Language Areas
    4. Kitchen/Bathroom Areas
