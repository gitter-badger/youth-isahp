Quick Start To Pairwise Project With Google Forms

Introduction

Welcome, if you are here then either you are considering participation in the Youth Session at ISAHP 2016 in London http://isahp.org, or you are interested in using the tool Dr. Adams wrote to get pairwise information from google forms and produce interesting information. In either case you can see an example of the results of using this process at Music Preference Example.
